About

It all started with a floppy disk chess game, on to an insatiable appetite for playing StarCraft and Diablo. HTML Goodies helped me make websites for my favorite games and sports teams on GeoCities and Angelfire. I didn't use stylesheets or javascript. I had frame tags! Frame tags were the greatest web technology to me. I spent more time working on the HTML than the content; I was creating and working with things I liked, because it was fun. The websites I created regularly donned the Internet Explorer badges, fire/explosion GIFs, and ofcourse under construction images when I was in development mode.

I am a web developer working for GuRuStu in downtown Tulsa, Oklahoma. These days, I enjoy working on Nodejs, Ruby on Rails, and Wordpress projects.
